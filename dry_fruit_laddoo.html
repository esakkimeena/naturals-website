🥜 What Is Dry Fruit Laddu?

Dry Fruit Laddu is a nutritious traditional sweet made from premium nuts, dates, raisins, and pure ghee. It provides natural energy and is a healthier alternative to processed snacks.

🌿 Key Ingredients & Their Roles

  • Almonds – Rich in protein and healthy fats.
  • Cashews – Adds creaminess and essential nutrients.
  • Dates – Natural sweetener and energy source.
  • Pistachios – Rich in antioxidants.
  • Raisins – Supports digestion and adds sweetness.
  • Pure Ghee – Enhances taste and nutrition.

💪 Major Benefits

  • ⚡ Instant Energy Boost
  • 🥜 Rich In Protein & Healthy Fats
  • 🛡️ Supports Immunity
  • ❤️ Heart-Friendly Nutrients
  • 🌟 Healthy Alternative To Sweets

🛠️ How We Make It

We carefully select premium dry fruits, roast them lightly, blend them with dates and pure ghee, then hand-roll them into delicious laddus without artificial preservatives.

🍽️ How To Enjoy

  1. Eat 1-2 laddus daily.
  2. Best as a healthy snack.
  3. Can be enjoyed with milk.
  4. Perfect before or after workouts.
