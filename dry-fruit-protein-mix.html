🥜 What Is Dry Fruits Healthy Mix?

Dry Fruits Healthy Mix is a nutritious traditional drink made from premium dry fruit ingredients, dates, raisins,and more grains. It provides natural energy and is a healthier alternative to processed snacks.

🌿 Key Ingredients & Their Roles

  • Almonds – Rich in protein and healthy fats.
  • Cashews – Adds creaminess and essential nutrients.
  • Pistachios – Rich in antioxidants.
  • Walnuts – Provides omega-3 fatty acids and protein.
  • pumpkin Seeds – Rich in protein and healthy fats.
  • Sunflower Seeds – Provides essential nutrients and antioxidants.
  • Flax Seeds – High in fiber and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Chia Seeds – Rich in fiber, protein, and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Raisins – Supports digestion and adds sweetness.
  • Pure Ghee – Enhances taste and nutrition.
  • More and More ingredients – Provides fiber and essential nutrients.

🛠️ How We Make It

We carefully select premium dry fruits, roast them lightly, then blend them with dates and pure ghee, then hand-roll them into delicious laddus without artificial preservatives.
