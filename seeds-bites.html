🥜 What Is Seeds Bites?

Seeds Bites is a nutritious traditional snack made from premium seeds, nuts, and natural ingredients. It provides natural energy and is a healthier alternative to processed snacks.

🌿 Key Ingredients & Their Roles

  • Pumpkin Seeds – Rich in protein and healthy fats.
  • Sunflower Seeds – Provides essential nutrients and antioxidants.
  • Flax Seeds – High in fiber and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Chia Seeds – Supports digestion and heart health.
  • Sesame Seeds – Adds crunch and is rich in minerals.
  • Watermelon Seeds – Boosts energy and supports overall health.
  • Pure Ghee – Enhances taste and nutrition.

💪 Major Benefits

  • ⚡ Instant Energy Boost
  • 🥜 Rich In Protein & Healthy Fats
  • 🛡️ Supports Immunity
  • ❤️ Heart-Friendly Nutrients
  • 🌟 Healthy Alternative To Sweets

🛠️ How We Make It

We carefully select premium dry seeds, roast them lightly, blend them with pure ghee, then hand-roll them into delicious laddus without artificial preservatives.

🍽️ How To Enjoy

  1. Eat 1-2 laddus daily.
  2. Best as a healthy snack.
  3. Can be enjoyed with milk.
  4. Perfect before or after workouts.
