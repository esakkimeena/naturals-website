🌹 What Is Sandalwood Soap?

Sandalwood Soap is a cleansing bar infused with sandalwood extracts, oils, petals, or sandalwoodwater. It cleanses, nourishes, soothes, and refreshes the skin with natural floral goodness. Unlike ordinary soaps, quality sandalwood soaps use natural ingredients and avoid harsh chemicals, making them gentle on the skin.

🌿 Key Ingredients & Their Roles

  • Sandalwood Extract / Sandalwood Oil / Sandalwoodwater – Soothes and balances skin tone.
  • Natural Oils (Coconut, Olive, Almond) – Deep moisturization.
  • Aloe Vera, Honey, Glycerin – Hydrates and protects skin.
  • Vitamin E & Antioxidants – Supports skin repair and glow.

✨ Major Benefits

  • 🌼 Gentle but effective cleansing
  • 💧 Deep hydration and moisture lock
  • 🌸 Soothes irritation and redness
  • ✨ Brightens skin and supports renewal
  • 🌿 Natural floral fragrance for relaxation
  • 🧴 Suitable for most skin types

🛁 How To Use

  1. Wet your skin with water.
  2. Lather the soap in your hands or loofah.
  3. Massage gently in circular motions.
  4. Rinse thoroughly with clean water.
  5. Pat dry with a soft towel.
  6. Use daily for best results.
